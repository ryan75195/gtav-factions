## The Story

After the events of *The Big Score*, the old guard of Los Santos crime crumbled. The FIB's attention shifted elsewhere, Merryweather retreated to lick its wounds, and the Triads vanished into the shadows. What remained was a city ripe for the taking.

**Michael De Santa**, flush with millions and bored of retirement, saw an opportunity to build something lasting — a legitimate empire built on the bones of the old criminal order. His methodical approach and connections made him a natural kingpin.

**Trevor Philips**, never one to be left behind, declared Sandy Shores the capital of his new empire. *Trevor Philips Industries* expanded beyond meth and guns into full territorial warfare. If Michael could be a crime lord, Trevor reasoned he could be a *war* lord.

**Franklin Clinton**, the youngest of the three, realized that loyalty to either friend would mean war with the other. Instead, he chose himself. Starting from Grove Street — the old Johnson family territory — Franklin built the *Clinton Organization* from the ground up.

The uneasy alliance of the Union Depository heist shattered. Now, three armies clash across Los Santos and Blaine County. Strip clubs, ports, airports, and neighborhoods have become battlegrounds.

**There can only be one Kingpin.**

## Features

### Choose Your Faction

Each protagonist leads their faction with a distinct AI personality:

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/faction-selection-placeholder.png" alt="Faction Selection Screen" width="600">
</p>

| Leader | Faction | AI Playstyle | Characteristics |
|--------|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Michael De Santa** | The De Santa Family | Defensive strategist | Fortifies high-value zones, strikes methodically |
| **Trevor Philips** | Trevor Philips Industries | Aggressive berserker | Relentless attacks, prioritizes combat over defense |
| **Franklin Clinton** | Clinton Organization | Balanced opportunist | Adapts to threats, responds to opportunities |

**Michael's AI** plays the long game — fortifying positions, protecting valuable territories, and striking only when victory is certain.

**Trevor's AI** overwhelms through sheer aggression — attacking relentlessly with little regard for defensive positioning.

**Franklin's AI** adapts to any situation — balanced decision-making lets him respond to threats and opportunities with equal effectiveness.

### Combat System

When factions clash over territory, war breaks out.

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/combat-placeholder.png" alt="Street Combat" width="700">
</p>

#### How Battles Work

1. **Enter an enemy zone** to trigger combat
2. **Eliminate all defenders** — reinforcements arrive in waves
3. **Zone becomes neutral** after victory
4. **Press E to claim** the zone for a small fee

#### Defender Tiers

Recruit different classes of soldiers for your army:

| Tier | Cost | Equipment | Combat Rating |
|------|------|-----------|---------------|
| **Basic** | $200 | Pistol, Light Armor | 1.0x |

| **Heavy** | $1,000 | Carbine, Full Armor | 2.0x |
| **Elite** | $2,000 | RPG, Full Armor | 2.5x |

### Recruitment & Your Squad

Build your personal army through two systems:

#### Zone Defenders

Purchase troops and **allocate them to defend your territories**. When enemies attack, your defenders will fight to hold the zone.

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/defender-menu-placeholder.png" alt="Defender Recruitment Menu" width="500">
</p>

#### Personal Bodyguards

Recruit **bodyguards who follow you into battle**. Your personal squad fights alongside you wherever you go.

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/bodyguard-squad-placeholder.png" alt="Bodyguard Squad" width="600">

### Zone Commander

Each zone you control has a **Commander NPC** — a friendly soldier who patrols your territory.

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/commander-placeholder.png" alt="Zone Commander" width="500">
</p>

- Look for the **blue blip** on your minimap
- Approach the Commander and **press E** to open the faction menu
- Commanders are armed and will **fight alongside your defenders** during attacks
- If killed, they **respawn automatically**

### Economy

Your war machine runs on **GTA V money** — the same cash you use throughout the game.

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/economy-placeholder.png" alt="Resource Overview" width="500">
</p>

- **Purchase troops** with your in-game cash
- **Buy vehicles** from the military shop
- **Claim neutral zones** for a small fee

AI factions generate income from their controlled zones and spend it to recruit troops and launch attacks. More zones under AI control means stronger opposition!

---

### AI Opponents

Enemy factions are controlled by intelligent AI that adapts to the war:

- **Michael's AI** plays defensively, protecting high-value zones
- **Trevor's AI** attacks relentlessly with little regard for losses
- **Franklin's AI** balances offense and defense strategically

<p align="center">
  <img src="docs/images/ai-attack-placeholder.png" alt="AI Faction Attacking" width="600">
</p>

The AI evaluates:
- Zone strategic value
- Available troops
- Your defensive strength
- Current territorial position

**Difficulty levels** affect AI income and resource tick rate:

| Difficulty | AI Income | Tick Interval |
|------------|-----------|---------------|
| Easy | 75% | 7 minutes |
| Normal | 100% | 5 minutes |
| Hard | 125% | 3 minutes |

Faster tick intervals mean AI factions generate resources and recruit troops more frequently.

## Installation

### Prerequisites
- GTA V (PC version)
- [ScriptHookV](http://www.dev-c.com/gtav/scripthookv/)
- [ScriptHookVDotNet](https://github.com/scripthookvdotnet/scripthookvdotnet/releases)

### Setup
1. Install ScriptHookV and ScriptHookVDotNet following their respective instructions
2. Copy `FactionWars.dll` to your GTA V `scripts` folder
3. Launch the game and press **F7** to open the menu

- Saves faction state, territories, troops, and relationships

## Building from Source

<details>
<summary>Developer Instructions</summary>

### Requirements
- .NET Framework 4.8 SDK
- Visual Studio 2022 or later
- ScriptHookVDotNet3.dll (place in `lib/` folder)

### Build Steps
```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/gtav-factions.git
cd gtav-factions

# Restore packages and build
dotnet build FactionWars.sln

# Run tests
dotnet test tests/FactionWars.Tests/FactionWars.Tests.csproj
```

### Architecture
- **SOLID principles** throughout
- **Test-Driven Development** with 2600+ tests
- **Dependency Injection** for all components
- **IGameBridge** abstraction for GTA V native calls

</details>
